## Lazy Days RV - formerly RVzz Repair Shop

Customer complaints about Lazydays RV of St.  George (formerly RVzz Repair Shop) regarding warranty work and communication include:

### Warranty Work Issues:
Multiple customers report that Lazydays does not honor warranties on RVs not purchased from them, even for manufacturer-backed coverage.  Some describe the warranty process as a "joke," with labor and diagnostics charged even when parts are covered. There are claims that technicians misdiagnose issues to inflate repair costs—such as one customer charged $700 for a generator diagnosis that turned out to be a loose wire and faulty spark plug. 
### Communication Problems:
Customers frequently cite lack of communication unless they initiate contact.  One review noted it took over a week to get a response to calls or texts, forcing them to contact corporate offices for follow-up.  Service managers are reported to not return calls, and updates on repair progress are often delayed or nonexistent. 

These issues have led to frustration over prolonged repair timelines, unresolved warranty claims, and a perceived lack of accountability post-acquisition.
